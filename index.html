About Apex Apparel Group

Founded in 2015, Apex Apparel Group has established itself as Ireland's premier clothing
manufacturer, specializing in high-quality streetwear and contemporary fashion. Based in
the heart of Dublin, our 50,000 square foot facility combines cutting-edge manufacturing
technology with traditional craftsmanship to deliver exceptional garments for brands
across Europe and beyond.

We pride ourselves on our flexibility and speed-to-market approach. Whether you're a
startup brand looking to produce your first collection or an established label seeking a
reliable manufacturing partner, Apex Apparel Group offers end-to-end solutions from
initial design consultation and sampling to full-scale production and quality control.

Our team of 80+ skilled professionals brings decades of combined experience in garment
construction, pattern making, and textile innovation. We work with premium fabrics
sourced from certified suppliers and maintain strict quality standards throughout every
stage of production. With minimum order quantities starting at just 100 units per style, we
make professional-grade manufacturing accessible to brands of all sizes.
